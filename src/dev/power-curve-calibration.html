📊 Calibration Test Overview

During this test: You WILL physically shift through 8 gears (34/28 → 50/13). Each gear gets 10 seconds to shift/stabilize, then 10 seconds recording. Total time: ~3 minutes.

During workouts: You'll stay locked in 34/17 and use virtual shifting (keyboard arrows) based on this calibration data.

  1. Starting Gear: Begin in 34/28 (small ring, easiest cog)
  2. Cadence: Pedal at your natural, comfortable cadence for each gear:
    • Easy gears (34/28, 34/23): expect higher cadence (90-110 RPM)
    • Mid gears (34/21, 50/21): optimal cadence (80-90 RPM)
    • Hard gears (50/17, 50/13): lower cadence (65-80 RPM)
    Don't force a fixed RPM! Let each gear find its natural rhythm.
  3. Test Flow: For each of 8 gears:
    • Instruction screen tells you which gear to shift to
    • 10 seconds to physically shift and stabilize cadence
    • 10 seconds recording power/cadence data
    • Total: ~3 minutes for complete test
